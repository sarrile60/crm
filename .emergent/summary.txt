The application is an internal CRM for the 1 LAW SOLICITORS legal firm, designed to manage leads and internal operations with strict, role-based access control.

**Core Features:**
*   **User Roles:** Admin, Supervisor, Agent. Admins have full access. Supervisors and Agents are restricted to leads within their assigned team or assigned to them directly.
*   **Lead Management:** A central dashboard to view, filter, and manage leads.
    *   Admins can import/export leads via CSV. This functionality is hidden from Supervisors and Agents.
    *   Leads created by Agents or Supervisors are automatically assigned to their team and to themselves.
*   **Phone Security:** Phone numbers are masked for non-admin roles (e.g., ) and are clickable  links.

    *   Notifications automatically disappear when the agent either changes the lead's status or reschedules the callback time.
