- **Real-Time Chat:** A floating chat bubble for internal communication.
    - Supports team chats and direct messages with file uploads and @mentions.
    - Hierarchical permissions (Agent < Supervisor < Admin).
    - Admins can view all conversations.
    - Toast notifications for new messages and unread count badges.
